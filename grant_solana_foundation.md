## Summary

OdoSeal is a decentralized vehicle data verification protocol built on Solana. It connects physical OBD-II hardware directly to the Solana blockchain — creating tamper-proof, permanent, publicly verifiable vehicle records.

Every mile. Every reading. On-chain. Forever.

---

## The Problem

- **Odometer fraud** costs consumers billions annually
- No open, decentralized standard for vehicle history verification
- Existing solutions (Carfax, dealerships) are centralized, expensive, and editable
- Vehicle data is owned by corporations — not users

---

## Why Solana

Solana is the only blockchain that can handle real-time vehicle data at scale:

| Requirement | Why Solana |
|---|---|
| Speed | Sub-second finality for real-time OBD-II streaming |
| Cost | Near-zero fees — critical for per-reading on-chain records |
| Scale | Thousands of vehicles writing simultaneously |
| Ecosystem | USDC payments, DeFi, NFT certificates |

OdoSeal is **natively built on Solana** — not a migration, not a bridge.

---

## How It Works

```
Vehicle OBD-II Port
      ↓ Bluetooth
OdoSeal Android App
      ↓ AES-256 encryption
Odokey (IPFS storage)
      ↓ CID
Solana Blockchain
— Immutable record
— Public verification
— User-owned data
```
