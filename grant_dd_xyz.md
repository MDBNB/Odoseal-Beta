## The Problem

Odometer fraud costs used car buyers **billions of dollars annually**. There is currently no open, tamper-proof, decentralized standard for verifying a vehicle's real history.

Centralized solutions like Carfax are:
- Expensive and restricted
- Editable by bad actors
- Inaccessible in emerging markets
- Owned by corporations, not users

---

## The Solution

OdoSeal is a **sovereign DePIN protocol** that connects physical vehicle hardware (OBD-II) directly to decentralized storage and blockchain — creating an immutable, public, user-owned vehicle history.

**The flow:**
```
Vehicle ECU (OBD-II)
      ↓ Bluetooth
OdoSeal Android App
      ↓
Odokey — AES-256 encrypted storage on IPFS
      ↓
Solana — tamper-proof on-chain CID record
```

No central authority. No editable database. No trust required.
